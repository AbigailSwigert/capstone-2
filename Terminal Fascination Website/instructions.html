Game Instructions

  • Open your computer's terminal

  • Enter the command java -jar TerminalFascination.jar

  • When prompted, type in your commands and hit <Enter> on your keyboard

    • For instance, when asked for your name, type in whatever collection of letters, symbols, and numbers you wish for your character to be referred to as

    • Or when you see a prompt with a list of numbers corresponding to actions, like this:
      0. Quit game
      1. Continue
      2. Go somewhere else
      Type the number corresponding to the action you want to take and hit <Enter> on your keyboard

  • Take your time to get to know all the characters in the game, as they may give you hints to what choices to make later on

  • If you wish to quit the game, you may enter 0 into the terminal at any time, and the game will end [WARNING: this command will delete your save data, you will not be able to come back to where you left off]
