The Making of Terminal Fascination

Terminal Fascination was created by Abigail Swigert as a capstone project for the TEKcamp bootcamp in July of 2020. The purpose of the project was for her to showcase not only her understanding of the Java language, but of OOP, and SOLID design principles.
